{{#t}}Because of your notification settings, Slack will remain in your taskbar even when closed.{{/t}}

{{#t}}To change this, go to Notifications → Sound & appearance. Once there, you can either…

  • Turn off the setting to flash the Slack window when receiving a notification.
  • Or leave the setting on, but check the option to only flash the window when you’re not using your computer.
{{/t}}
