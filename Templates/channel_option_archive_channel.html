Archiving a channel is useful to clean things up when you do not anticipate using the channel any more. If you archive this channel:

  • No one will be able to send messages to it anymore
  • It will be closed for anyone who has it open and all members will be removed
  • You will be able to view past conversations in the Archives on the site
  • You will be able to search for archived messages from this channel
  • You will always be able to un-archive it later
